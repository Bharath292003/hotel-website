About Hotel Narikootam

Launched in 1975, Narikootam's Hotel Group is the hospitality business of HNT Limited. With over 100 hotels in 70 destinations across six distinct brands, Narikootam’s hotel group integrates India’s renowned tradition of hospitality and warmth, with globally benchmarked facilities & services.

The safest 5-star hotel in Chennai with a unique AllSAFE hygiene label in ECR/OMR. Featuring 3-acres of greenery, city-view & greenery facing rooms, two 24-hrs gyms, 6+sports, 2 International restaurants, Asian & Japanese Ramen bar, 8-mpbs Wi-fi, 24 hrs multi-lingual team, doctor on call, an outstanding breakfast buffet, 24-hrs Check-in/Check-out. The outdoor pool is surrounded by lush gardens. Weekend well-being & fitness activities. Ideal for business, families or a city break.
